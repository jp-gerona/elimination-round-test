Introducing

Mapúa MCL Flexible Learning Ubiquitous Experience or “Mapúa MCL FLUX” integrates traditional classroom instruction with online learning, providing students with the flexibility to choose how they engage with each class and learning activity.

Face-to-Face

MMCL's facilities are fully equipped with state-of-the-art gadgets and learning aids, providing students access to educational-technology tools while they learn inside the campus.

Synchronous

Students can actively participate in synchronous classes and collaborate with their peers and instructors in real-time through applications such as Microsoft Teams, Blackboard Online, and many more.

Asynchronous

Students can learn at their own pace inside the comfort of their own homes by accessing instructional materials and libraries provided by MMCL, in partnership with Wiley, Blackboard Online, and many more.

Whether it's in-person, in online sessions, or at their own pace, our students have the freedom to tailor their educational journey to their preferences, thanks to technology.
